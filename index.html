BraQIIIT

The Quantum Lab at IIIT-D.

BraQIIIT is a research group led by Dr. Debajyoti Bera at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi (IIIT-Delhi). Broad research interests of this group include quantum query complexity, quantum circuit complexity, and quantum algorithms for cryptography, machine learning and optimization.
