BraQIIIT

The Quantum Lab at IIIT-Delhi.

BraQIIIT is a research group led by Dr. Debajyoti Bera at the Computer Science department of Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi (IIIT-Delhi). Broad research interests of this group include quantum query complexity, quantum circuit complexity, and quantum algorithms for cryptography, machine learning and optimization.

Useful Resources

Online Resources

  • Qiskit Textbook
    An interactive online textbook for an introduction to quantum computing and programming.
  • Quantum Country
    A very descriptive but limited introduction to the basics of quantum computing.
  • Quantum Algorithm Zoo
    An assortment of important quantum algorithms, all in one place.

Text Books

  • Quantum Computing for Computer Scientists by Yanofsky and Mannucci
    Basic introduction to QC from a more computer science perspective.
  • Introduction to Classical and Quantum Computing by Thomas Wong
    Focuses on the basics of QC, tools like linear algebra and how to connect them.
  • Quantum Computation and Quantum Information by Neilson and Chuang
    The Bible of Quantum Computing. Needs knowledge of some linear algebra.
  • An Introduction to Quantum Computing by Kaye, Laflamme and Mosca (Advanced)
    Expected to be read after some familiarity with the basics of QC and linear algebra.
